The creative layer that gives form, consistency and velocity to the entire FBR ecosystem.

01 • Product Vision

A studio platform, not a task board.

The platform exists to organize creative demand as business infrastructure: every request carries context, every deliverable carries lineage, every approval carries accountability, and every final asset can return to the ecosystem as reusable visual capital.

Creative governance

Requests move through defined workflow gates with review history, explicit approvals, SLA awareness and auditability.

Brand consistency at scale

Templates, guidelines, collections and brand systems keep every project aligned without slowing production.

Reusable outputs

Deliverables are stored as reusable assets with versions, derivations, tags, campaign context and future retrieval value.

03 • Main Flow

From brief to reusable asset.

The system structures the visual lifecycle as an operational sequence, not a loose collection of files and comments.

Step 01

Intake

Requests arrive from internal teams and FBR systems with brand, campaign, objective and urgency context.

Step 02

Triage

The platform classifies the demand, assigns priority, selects the right workflow and opens the request.

Step 03

Production

Creative tasks, previews, references and deliverables evolve with explicit statuses and dependency control.

Step 04

Review

Feedback becomes structured history, enabling rework without losing decision context or version traceability.

Step 05

Delivery & Library

Final outputs are approved, delivered, archived and indexed as reusable visual assets for future requests.

04 • Ecosystem Integrations

Designed to orbit every FBR operation.

`FBR-Design` becomes more valuable as it connects to the rest of the ecosystem, especially the mature commercial operation already evolving inside `FBR-Sales`.

Integration priorities

  • `FBR-Sales`: campaigns, bundles, proposals, media kits and reusable commercial assets
  • `FBR-MKT`: branding, positioning, campaign systems and strategic materials
  • `FBR-Video`: hybrid pieces, thumbnail systems and audiovisual identity support
  • `FBR-Redacao`: copy-dependent creative production and editorial materials
  • `FBR-Finance`: cost, vendors and external production traceability
  • `FBR-Dev`: storage, automations, AI assistance and integrations
